Kevin has also received many awards throughout his lifetime. In 2012, Kevin won the Best Actor award at the BET Awards for the comedy show Kevin Hart: Laugh at My Pain. Kevin has also won awards such as Outstanding Comedy Series, Entertainer of the Year, Comedic Genius Award, and Top Comedy Tour. Kevin has won other awards than these and has been nominated for many others.
