Gabriel Iglesias was born July 15th, 1976 in San Diego, California. He was the youngest of six children of Esther and Jesús. Gabriel was raised by his mother, and never really knew his father until his adult years. Growing up he lived in Riverside, Corona, Santa Ana, Baldwin Park, and Compten before settling in Long Beach where he spent most of his youth. During his childhood he developed a strong sense of humor to help cope with the obstacles he faced. In 1997 he sat out to hone his skills and become a comedian and perform stand-up wherever he could find an audience. Gabriel's stand-up includes a variety of elements including things about his personal life, paradies, characters, and sound effects that bring his show to life.

When Iglesias became intrested in comedy. He began performing at local clubs while still working for a cell phone company during the day. While encouraged not to give up his day job, he eventually did to work on his act full time. His career started off slow. He was evicted from his home and his car was repossessed. In 2000, Iglesias landed his first break on television where he became part of the cast of a Nickelodean sketch comedy. The show was called All That. In 2003, Gabriel landed a half hour comedian special on comedy central which put him in greater demand as a comedian.
